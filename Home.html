Design IA

The Design Technology Internal Assessment (IA) entails a comprehensive understanding of the evaluation criteria and their visual representation. It is essential for students to grasp the criteria outlined by the International Baccalaureate (IB) in order to successfully complete this assessment. The IA requires students to demonstrate their knowledge and skills in various areas, including research, design development, testing, and evaluation. These criteria ensure that students develop a well-rounded understanding of design technology principles.
